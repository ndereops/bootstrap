These Are Some Of The Top Rated National Parks And Reserves In Kenya

Nairobi national Park

Nairobi National Park is a national park in Kenya that was established in 1946 about 7 km south of Nairobi.

Masai Mara National Park

Maasai Mara National Reserve is an area of preserved savannah wilderness in southwestern Kenya, along the Tanzanian border.

Tsavo National Park

Tsavo is situated in a semi-arid area previously known as the Taru Desert in taita taveta area.

Meru National Park

Meru National Park is a Kenyan national park located east of Meru, 350 km from Nairobi. Covering an area of 870 km², it is one best known national parks in Kenya.

more information
Mt.kenya National Park

Mount Kenya National Park was established in 1949 to protect Mount Kenya, the wildlife and surrounding environment, which forms a habitat for wild animals, as well as acting as an area for the catchment of water.

Nakuru National Park

Lake Nakuru National Park is a birdwatcher's paradise as well as a place rich with wildlife for the Kenya safari-goers.
